Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1 BALI ARRIVAL– SUNSET DINNER CRUISE (D)

Upon arrival at International Airport of Bali, meet & greet then directly transferred to your chosen hotel for check in. In the afternoon you will be picked up at Hotel lobby to Join Sunset Dinner Cruise. There’s no better way to spend a balmy tropical evening than on Evening Dinner Cruise in the SEA SAFARI CRUISE. As the sun goes down we set off on a relaxing cruise around the harbour. Be entertained by our live musical entertainers at the bridge deck, savour a delicious international buffet dinner, then dance the night away at our disco or enjoy our live cabaret show. Return to hotel.

Day 2 FULL DAY DENPASAR CITY TOUR – UBUD (B)

Breakfast at hotel. This tour visits the BALI MUSEUM with its grand architecture housing artifacts from antiquity. Continue Photo stop in BAJRA SANDI Monument Renon. Next visit BATUBULAN stone figures on the roadside mark the village as the center of stone carving. Divinities and demons are carved from sandstone, limestone or volcanic stone to be used in houses and temples, or to be placed before entrances and road intersections. Continue to CELUK, famous for it is silver and gold smith also the place where you may see traditional Balinese house compound, next proceed to MAS: the home of Bali’s talented woodcarver and well known for special quality woodcarving and others handicraft, then UBUD: worldwide known as Bali’s heart of art & craft especially painting. Last stop is UBUD MARKET. Back to hotel.

Day 3 FULL DAY  BEDUGUL - TANAH LOT TOUR (B)

Breakfast at hotel. The tour will visit TAMAN AYUN Royal Family Temple in Mengwi, then drive up to BEDUGUL-an attractive mountain resort located about 1100 meters above sea level. There lies tropical market and an ancient Lake Beratan with its magnificent “floating” Ulun Danu Temple dedicated to the goddess of the water dwelling in this area. After lunch drive down to TANAH LOT-a spectacular temple built in the 17th century, on top of rock about 200 meters offshore. The whole compound will turn into a wonderful silhouette as the sunset right behind the temple, one of the most photographed temples on the island - for good reason.  Back to hotel for accommodation.

Day 4 FREE PROGRAM (B)

Breakfast serve at hotel. Free program at your own leisure

Day 5 BALI - DEPARTURE (B)

Breakfast at hotel, then transfer to the airport for your onward flight.
